# Treatment plan experience — release notes (draft)

---

## What's new

### Check off treatments during a visit

Providers can now mark any plan item as completed directly from the client detail panel as treatments are performed. Tap the checkbox to the left of any item. Completed items appear with a strikethrough so it's clear at a glance what's been done and what's still ahead.

### Completed items are excluded from the shared treatment plan link

When a provider shares a treatment plan link with a patient, items already marked as completed are automatically excluded. The patient only sees what's still planned — not treatments they've already received.

### Completed status carries through to the treatment recommender

In the treatment recommender view, items that have been marked Built show a ✓ indicator and are visually de-emphasized, so providers can quickly see a client's history without it cluttering the active plan.

### Wishlist prices are not shown to the patient

When sharing a treatment plan, wishlist item prices are not included in the patient-facing link. Patients see the wishlist as aspirational options, not a line-item quote.

### Other procedures in the plan builder

An "Other procedures" section will surface uncategorized treatments like PRFM and Skinvive directly in the plan builder so providers don't have to work around their absence.

### Per-client price editing before sharing

Providers will be able to adjust prices on individual line items before sending the treatment plan link to a patient, without changing the practice's base pricing.

### Scheduling treatments for a specific date

Plan items will support an optional target date so providers can work backwards from a patient's event or deadline — a wedding, a vacation, a procedure — and ensure every treatment is scheduled in time.

### Area labels on each plan item

Each treatment in the shared plan will include the specific area being treated (e.g. "Botox — forehead and glabella") so patients and providers have full context everywhere.

### Skincare quiz emoji sync to Boulevard

When a client completes the skincare quiz, their resulting skin emoji is automatically written to their Boulevard record. This is built and ready — it just needs sign-off before it's switched on.
